Corporate

Invitation

The Central Placement Cell, University of Delhi has entered the eighth year of its inception. All the seven batches registered with CPC in past have duly benefited from Industry- Students interaction. Corporate houses from different sectors have participated in our endeavor and recruited large number of students from the university and its colleges during these seven years. We express our gratitude towards these companies for reposing faith in our students and selecting them. We further wish to nurture this relationship by extending the data base to include Ph.D/Post Graduate/Under Graduate students along with the students from School of Open Learning/Non-Collegiate Women Education Board. This pool extends to all Delhi University Colleges and Post Graduate Departments. The CPC certainly ensures a wider choice in terms of number and quality of students available at a single platform. The vision of CPC is to provide a continuum of opportunities to the undergraduate, post graduate students and research scholars studying in various courses of Science (Physical, Life and Applied sciences), Mathematical Sciences, Commerce and Social Sciences. A detailed list of courses offered by the University of Delhi is available on the university website. The Central Placement Cell seeks the support of all corporate houses and NGO’s in our endeavor to provide suitable placements to our students and help them in their competencies enhancement. University of Delhi also appreciates corporates in their endeavor to encourage recruitment of Differently Abled Students. We also invite your company to participate in Internship Programme for I & II year students. We would be extremely happy if you / your organization would come forward and support the initiative of the University as an esteemed partner in this activity. A very large number of students form different Colleges of Delhi University and Departments are registered under one umbrella. The CPC Corporate registration form is available online at http://placement.du.ac.in. The registration fee of Rs.10,000/- (Ten Thousand only) is payable by Cheque or Demand Draft in the name of Registrar, University of Delhi after online registration. The registration is valid for one academic year. The hard copy of the filled registration form may be sent to the following address at your earliest convenience.
